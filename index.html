Necro Chess

Command the undead in this dark fantasy chess variant where death is just the beginning.

Game Overview

After struggling to live with no job, a girl becomes a necromancer and tries to overthrow the King.

In this unique chess variant, you'll command an army of undead warriors in a dark fantasy setting. Combine classic chess strategy with necromantic powers as you battle for supremacy in a gothic-themed battlefield.

Key Features

  • Unique undead-themed chess pieces
  • Dark fantasy atmosphere
  • Strategic gameplay mechanics
  • Cross-platform compatibility

How to Play

Though the game is designed to be played entirely with a mouse, here are the keyboard controls:

  • R Roll
  • SPACEBAR Attack / Pause Battle
  • S Sell chosen thing (or click and hold)
  • F Go to fullscreen (only on downloaded version)

Pro Tip: You can also FREEZE something by clicking on it twice!

Important Notes:

  • • Books need to be at the right of the lane to be able to be used.
  • • Merging units is important!
  • • The king gets slowly stronger, so try to rush him or go slow and become strong enough!
